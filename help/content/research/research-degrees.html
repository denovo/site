Research Degrees

  • Research Environment
  • Research Staff

The University of the Arts London offers both theoretical and practice-based research programmes leading to a PhD or MPhil awarded in any subject area where the University can provide appropriately qualified supervision. It also offers a research degree programme by Published Work which can be completed for both PhD and MPhil awards.

Research Degree Students benefit from full access to cross-College resources; interdisciplinary dialogue; and research student training via the Research Network University of the Arts London (RNUAL) which offers a comprehensive programme of support and training to enhance their studies and future careers as researchers.
