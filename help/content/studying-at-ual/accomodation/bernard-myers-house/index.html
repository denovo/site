Bernard Myers House is a hall with a great sense of community conveniently close to Camberwell College of Arts

Many practising artists and designers live in Camberwell, and itâ€™s easy to see why, with its cultural and ethnic diversity and strong sense of community. Bernard Myers House is very handy for Camberwell College of Arts, which is only a short walk away, and London College of Communication (Elephant & Castle) is just 20 minutes by bus. This hall is managed by Unite Student Accommodation for the exclusive use of University of the Arts London students. Bernard Myers House is signed up to the ANUK code of practice.

Your tenancy at Bernard Myers House will be with UNITE group plc. As such your conditions of residence will be with them rather than the University. Before you apply please read UNITE's Terms & Conditions of residence. (UNITE will send these with any offer of accommodation).

Bernard Myers House is located in Zone 2, 5 minutes walk from Camberwell College of Arts and a short bus ride from London College of Communication and Chelsea College of Art and Design. Very close to the newly redeveloped Burgess Park, residents also have easy access to the heart of London.

The immediate local area also benefits from a small supermarket, independent coffee shops, a great local pub and various fast food take away restaurants. An average journey time in to Oxford Circus is 30 - 45 minutes. Camberwell Green is served by many bus routes that run in and out of London 24 hours a day. The nearest overground train stations are Peckham Rye or Denmark Hill. The nearest tube stations are Oval (Northern Line) or Elephant and Castle (Bakerloo and Northern Line).
