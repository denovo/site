We are the only specialist arts, design and creative industries awarding body in the UK. We pride ourselves on our knowledge of the sectors we work in, and the quality and integrity of the qualifications we award.

The UK has world-class creative industries, and we are here to support them by helping to develop the creative workforce of the future.

UAL Awarding Body has been approved by Ofqual, the independent body that regulates qualifications in England, to award qualifications to 14-19 year olds and adults. All our qualifications are accredited to the Qualifications & Credit Framework (QCF) and the majority are eligible for funding by the Education Funding Agency (EFA) and Skills Funding Agency (SFA).

Fit-for-purpose qualifications

We respond to the needs of the education sector and the creative industries, and develop and award qualifications in a way that meets their needs. We work collaboratively to ensure that our qualifications provide students with opportunities to develop the skills, knowledge and understanding to progress into further education or training, higher education and the workplace.

The quality of our qualifications is maintained via a robust and rigorous process of quality assurance, beginning with a thorough Centre approval process and culminating in the use of fit-for-purpose assessment. All of our qualifications are assessed through a visit from an expert external moderator or verifier, recruited and trained to ensure that assessment decisions are valid, fair and reliable.
