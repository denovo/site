Global Color Codes and Classnames

UAL Default Colors

  • ual-black

    SASS: $ual-black

    HEX: #000000

    RGB: (0,0,0)

    notes:

    Text sizes above 16px.

  • soft-black

    SASS: $soft-black

    HEX: #282828

    RGB: (40,40,40)

    notes:

    Body text 16px and below.

  • dark-gray

    SASS: $dark-gray

    HEX: #333333

    RGB: (51,51,51)

    notes:

    Visual elements on black only.

    Not for use with overlayed text.

  • aaa-gray

    SASS: $aaa-gray

    HEX: #757575

    RGB: (117,117,117)

    notes:

    Passes AA rating for bold text at 14px

    and AAA rating for regular text at 18px & up on both ual white and ual black.

  • college-logo-gray

    SASS: $college-logo-gray

    HEX: #999999

    RGB: (153,153,153)

    notes:

    College logo lockup text only. Passes AAA rating on ual black.

    Passes AA rating for bold text at 14px and 18px and up on ual white.

  • light-gray

    SASS: $light-gray

    HEX: #c8c8c8

    RGB: (200,200,200)

    notes:

    Visual elements on white only.

    Not for use with overlayed text.

  • ual-white

    SASS: $ual-white

    HEX: #FFFFFF

    RGB: (255,255,255)

    notes:

    Text on ‘ual black’ or ‘ual AAA gray’.
