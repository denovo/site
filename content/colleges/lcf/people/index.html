Our researchers

LCF staff have extensive research, consultancy and professional experience within a wide range of specialist areas and enjoy close links with the media and fashion industries.

Research activity within the fashion media and cultural field encompasses curation and exhibition of fashion and fashion photography at international venues, contributions to the global fashion press, editorships of major research and trade journals, and well established links with museums such as the V&A.
