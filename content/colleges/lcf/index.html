Better Lives

Using fashion to drive change, build a sustainable future and improve the way we live.

Better Lives is a term we use here at London College of Fashion to describe the work we do which uses fashion, as a discipline, to drive change, build a sustainable future and improve the way we live.

Film Series

LCF Men is a series of seven short films produced by London College of Fashion to mark the launch of London Collections: Men, the kick-start to the menswear calendar. Released throughout January 2013, the films feature a selection of Britain’s most stylish men. Watch all of the LCF Men films.
