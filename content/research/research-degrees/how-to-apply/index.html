Applying

  1. Once you are ready to apply, please download the application form and follow the guidance for applying.

  2. Email completed application forms to: researchdegrees@arts.ac.uk.

  3. You will receive a reply email from this address confirming receipt of your application within 5 working days.

    If you do not receive this confirmation within this time, please contact this office using the above email address without re-attaching your application or call this office on +44 (0)207 514 8187.

    Please also ensure that the email containing your application form and supporting documents do not exceed a total combined size of 7MB, as otherwise it will not be delivered.
