Karin Askham
Dean of the School of Media

As Dean of the School of Media at LCC, Karin Askham leads undergraduate and postgraduate courses in photography, sound, interactive media, animation, film & television, media theory, journalism, PR and publishing.

Follow this link to go to more information aboutView Karins profile

The ethos of the School of Media is learning by doing. Our student journalists produce a weekly newspaper and associated website and our publishing students have won international awards for their work.
